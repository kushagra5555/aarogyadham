Frozen Shoulder: Can Gentle Yoga Help You Move Again?

June 2025 · 4 min read

Frozen shoulder can make even small daily movements frustrating. Reaching overhead, turning in bed, wearing clothes, or lifting the arm away from the body can start to feel guarded and painful.

Why People Often Push Too Hard

Many people think they need aggressive stretching. But when the shoulder is already reactive, forcing range can increase fear and make the body protect itself even more.

How Supported Practice Can Help

Gentle Iyengar-based support uses props, positioning, and controlled movement to reduce strain while helping the shoulder explore motion more gradually. That often makes practice more tolerable and more repeatable.

What Makes Progress More Realistic

Useful progress usually comes from consistency, not force. The goal is to rebuild comfort, restore movement confidence, and reduce the defensive holding pattern around the shoulder.
