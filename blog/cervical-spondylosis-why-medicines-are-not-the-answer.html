Cervical Spondylosis: Why Medicines Alone Often Fall Short

April 2025 · 4 min read

Neck pain is often managed only at the symptom level. Short-term relief may help a difficult day, but it does not automatically change posture, work habits, movement confidence, or upper-back stiffness.

Why the Neck Keeps Getting Overloaded

Hours at screens, stress-driven shoulder tension, and limited thoracic mobility can all increase strain on the neck. Without changing those patterns, discomfort often returns.

What Supportive Yoga Can Add

Iyengar therapy helps slow the process down. It uses supported alignment, shoulder opening, upper-back work, and breathing patterns that reduce excess tension instead of feeding it.

Think Long-Term, Not Just Temporary

The real goal is better movement habits and less repeated overload. That is why personalised guidance matters more than copying exercises from social media or rushing into aggressive neck stretches.
