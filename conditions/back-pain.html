Yoga Therapy for Back Pain

Back pain often builds from posture strain, spinal compression, weak support muscles, fear of movement, or long periods of sitting. At Yog Arogyadham, Iyengar yoga therapy is used to improve alignment, decompress the back safely, and rebuild confidence step by step.

Who This Page Is For

This support page is useful for people dealing with chronic lower back pain, recurring stiffness, disc-related discomfort, posture fatigue, and back pain that returns after short-term relief.

How Iyengar Therapy Helps

  • Supported poses can reduce excess strain while restoring movement.
  • Prop-based sequencing helps improve spinal alignment gradually.
  • Breathwork and pacing help reduce guarding and fear around movement.

What a Plan May Include

A back pain plan may include supported standing work, traction-based postures, gentle extension, safe forward recovery work, and home guidance tailored to your current tolerance.

Next Steps
