Yoga Support for Frozen Shoulder

Frozen shoulder can make dressing, reaching, sleeping, and everyday independence feel frustrating. A careful plan needs to respect pain and stiffness while gently improving range, alignment, and confidence.

Common Difficulties

  • Pain when lifting the arm or reaching behind the back.
  • Shoulder stiffness that affects sleep and routine tasks.
  • Fear of movement because stretching often feels worse.

How Therapeutic Yoga Helps

Iyengar props and supported positioning can reduce strain while allowing the shoulder and upper back to open more gradually. This can make movement feel safer and more repeatable.

What a Plan May Include

A frozen shoulder plan may include chest opening, supported arm positioning, upper-back mobility work, wall support, breath pacing, and home guidance based on your current stage.
