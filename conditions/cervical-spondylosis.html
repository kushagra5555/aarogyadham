Cervical Spondylosis Support

Neck pain and cervical stiffness are often worsened by workstation posture, stress, poor movement habits, and repeated compression through the upper spine and shoulders.

Common Concerns

  • Morning stiffness and reduced neck rotation.
  • Upper-back tightness linked to screen time and posture.
  • Pain that keeps returning after short relief from medicines or rest.

Therapeutic Direction

Iyengar yoga therapy can help by improving shoulder placement, opening the upper back, and teaching safe head-neck alignment with support instead of force.

Practical Goal

The aim is not aggressive stretching. The goal is better alignment, steadier breathing, and safer movement habits you can continue outside the class.
