Yoga Support for Arthritis and Joint Stiffness

Arthritis and long-term joint stiffness can reduce mobility, confidence, and independence. A useful support plan has to improve movement quality without rushing painful joints or unstable positions.

Support Priorities

  • Reducing fear around movement through safer supported practice.
  • Improving joint-friendly alignment and pacing.
  • Building repeatable routines that support daily comfort and function.

Why the Iyengar Approach Matters

Props, wall support, and gradual sequencing can make yoga more accessible for people who cannot comfortably join a normal class format. The aim is better function, not force.

What a Plan May Include

An arthritis support plan may include chair-assisted work, supported standing, breath pacing, gentle range-of-motion work, and practical home routines that respect daily variability.
