Your Questions, Answered

What Is Medical Yoga Therapy and Who Is It For?

Medical yoga therapy at Yog Arogyadham uses precise BKS Iyengar postures, props and breathing techniques to reduce pain, improve alignment and support natural recovery. It is designed for people dealing with chronic back pain, sciatica, cervical or lumbar spondylitis, knee pain, stiffness, stress and other long-term mobility issues.

What conditions do you treat?

We treat 40+ therapeutic conditions including chronic back pain, sciatica, disc bulge, cervical pain, lumbar pain, knee discomfort, posture-related stiffness, hypertension, diabetes support, asthma and anxiety-related tension.

How does Iyengar yoga help chronic pain?

Iyengar yoga helps chronic pain by improving structural alignment, reducing compression, building safe strength and using props so each posture can be adapted for the body you have today, not the body you wish you had.

Why choose Yog Arogyadham in Dehradun and Haridwar?

Patients choose Yog Arogyadham for its BKS Iyengar lineage, two Uttarakhand centres, 15+ years of therapeutic experience and carefully guided programs built around individual conditions instead of one-size-fits-all routines.
